# [DMSG](../../README.md) / [Official Modules](../README.md) / [Strixhaven](README.md) / Nightfall

The College of Nightfall is a dark magic institution dedicated to the study of the occult and forbidden arts. The College of Nightfall is located in a secluded and mysterious corner of the Strixhaven campus, accessible only to those who have been specifically granted entry. The college is shrouded in secrecy and surrounded by a thick mist that obscures it from view.

## Faculty

The faculty of the College of Nightfall is comprised of highly-skilled and knowledgeable practitioners of dark magic, many of whom are rumored to have dabbled in the forbidden arts. These faculty members teach a wide range of courses, from the history of dark magic to the practical applications of spells and rituals. They also conduct research in various areas of dark magic, including necromancy, blood magic, and demonic summoning.

## Philosophy

The philosophy of the College of Nightfall is centered on the idea that dark magic, while dangerous and often taboo, can also be incredibly powerful and useful. They believe that by studying the darker aspects of magic, they can gain a deeper understanding of the nature of magic itself, and harness its power more effectively. However, they also recognize the dangers of unbridled pursuit of power, and emphasize the importance of responsible and ethical use of magic.

## Campus

The campus of the College of Nightfall is a sprawling complex of shadowy halls, dimly-lit classrooms, and arcane laboratories. The architecture is gothic and foreboding, with dark stone walls and ornate, twisted spires. The campus is constantly shrouded in an eerie mist, and the air is thick with the scent of incense and other mystical herbs. The college also has an extensive library of ancient tomes and grimoires, many of which are rumored to contain dangerous and forbidden knowledge.
