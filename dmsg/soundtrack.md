## Rating System

|Rating|Criteria|
|-|-|
|S|The same as A-tier, except specifically well suited for tabletop RPGs in composition.|
|A|The best of it's class, fulfills everything you'd expect from a track of it's type.|
|B|Better-than-C track, but either surpassed by better alternatives in S/A or lacks something specific. |
|C|Average track, great for most low-stakes situations.|
|D|A good track, but either too situational or silly to be higher.|
|F|A completely terrible track, because sometimes we need those?|
